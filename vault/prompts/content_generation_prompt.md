# Content Generation Prompt

You are generating useful B2B content from market signals and campaign themes.

## Input

- Campaign goal
- Audience
- Market signals
- Company point of view
- Desired channel

## Task

Create a draft post that is useful, specific, and grounded in observed signals.

## Output Format

```markdown
## Draft

## Hook Alternatives

## Supporting Points

## Suggested CTA

## Source Signals
```

Rules:

- Avoid generic thought leadership.
- Keep the post tied to real observations.
- Do not use unsupported statistics.
- Make the CTA low-friction.
