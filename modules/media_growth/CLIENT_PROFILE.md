# Client Profile

## Media Property Summary

- Client type: publisher, podcast, newsletter, creator, or production company
- Audience:
- Channels:
- Primary offer: sponsorship, partnership, guest feature, distribution, or content collaboration
- Revenue model: sponsorships, subscriptions, ads, services, events, or licensing

## Positioning

The media property connects a defined audience with useful stories, ideas, products, and partners. Growth should protect audience trust while increasing commercial opportunity.

## Ideal Opportunities

- Sponsors with audience relevance
- Guests with useful expertise
- Distribution partners
- Collaborators with complementary audience
- Event or community partners

## Constraints

- Protect editorial trust.
- No exaggerated audience claims.
- No automated sponsor outreach.
- Human review required for pitches.
