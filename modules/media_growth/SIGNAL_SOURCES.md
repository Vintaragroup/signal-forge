# Signal Sources

## Primary Sources

| Source | Data Collected | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sponsor websites | category, offer, campaign | sponsor fit |
| Newsletters and podcasts | audience, topics, partners | collaboration fit |
| Social posts | launches, events, hiring | timing signal |
| Event pages | sponsors, speakers, attendees | partnership signal |
| Press releases | product or funding news | outreach timing |
| Existing media kit | audience and inventory | offer alignment |

## Signal Types

- Product launch
- Funding or growth
- Event sponsorship
- Audience overlap
- Topic relevance
- Guest expertise
- Advertiser category fit

## Normalized Fields

- `source`
- `source_url`
- `target_name`
- `target_type`
- `company_name`
- `audience_fit`
- `opportunity_type`
- `signal`
- `recommended_offer`

## Source Rules

- Preserve audience trust.
- Store source attribution.
- Avoid private analytics collection.
- Human review required before outreach.
