# Outreach Templates

## Sponsor Outreach

```text
Hi, I found {{company_name}} while reviewing companies relevant to {{audience_description}}.

The signal that stood out was {{signal}}.

I think there may be a fit for {{recommended_offer}} because {{priority_reason}}.

Would it be useful to send the short sponsorship note?
```

## Guest Or Collaboration Pitch

```text
Hi, I noticed {{target_name}} has been talking about {{topic}}.

That overlaps with what our audience cares about: {{audience_description}}.

Would you be open to a short guest or collaboration idea?
```

## Follow-Up

```text
Hi, following up on the media partnership idea.

The reason I thought it could fit was {{priority_reason}}.

Should I send the short version?
```

## Rules

- Do not exaggerate audience size.
- Do not imply guaranteed outcomes.
- Protect editorial trust.
- Human review required.
