# Media Growth Module

This module defines a SignalForge strategy pack for publishers, podcasts, newsletters, creators, production teams, and media businesses that want to identify sponsors, partners, guests, collaborators, and audience growth opportunities.

## Module Focus

- Media business growth
- Sponsorship and partnership pipeline
- Guest, collaborator, and distribution opportunities
- Content strategy tied to audience signals

## Primary Use Cases

- Source sponsor or partner targets.
- Score opportunities by audience fit, timing, and value.
- Prepare human-reviewed partnership outreach.
- Track outreach lifecycle and reporting.

## Current Status

Documentation-first module. No media platform, ad sales, email, or social integrations have been added.
