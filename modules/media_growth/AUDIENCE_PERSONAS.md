# Audience Personas

## Sponsor Buyer

- Role: founder, marketer, partnerships lead, demand gen lead
- Goal: reach a relevant audience
- Pain points: unclear fit, low trust, generic inventory
- Buying triggers: campaign launch, new product, event, budget cycle
- Message angle: audience relevance and clear placement idea

## Editorial Guest

- Role: expert, founder, operator, creator
- Goal: share useful expertise
- Pain points: poor fit, vague topic, weak audience match
- Buying triggers: launch, book, funding, market news
- Message angle: specific topic and audience value

## Distribution Partner

- Role: community lead, newsletter operator, podcast host
- Goal: grow reach through aligned collaboration
- Pain points: unbalanced value exchange
- Buying triggers: campaign, event, audience overlap
- Message angle: clear collaboration idea

## Audience Member

- Role: reader, listener, viewer, subscriber
- Goal: learn or be entertained
- Pain points: low-quality sponsored content
- Buying triggers: relevant problem, timely topic
- Message angle: useful content before promotion
