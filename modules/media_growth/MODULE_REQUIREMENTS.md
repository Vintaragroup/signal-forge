# Media Module Requirements

## Objective

Help a media operator identify and prioritize sponsors, partners, guests, collaborators, and content distribution opportunities.

## Functional Requirements

- Define audience and media property profile.
- Define sponsor and partner personas.
- Track source signals.
- Score opportunities by audience relevance and timing.
- Prepare human-reviewed outreach.
- Track outreach lifecycle.
- Report pipeline health.

## Non-Goals

- No automated ad sales.
- No automated email sending.
- No scraping private analytics.
- No claims about guaranteed reach or conversions.

## Required Data

- Media property
- Audience profile
- Target company or person
- Opportunity type
- Source signal
- Audience fit
- Recommended offer
- Review status
- Outreach status

## Acceptance Criteria

- Audiences are clearly defined.
- Sponsor and partner fit rules are explainable.
- Outreach templates are specific and non-generic.
- KPIs connect to sponsor, partner, or audience outcomes.
