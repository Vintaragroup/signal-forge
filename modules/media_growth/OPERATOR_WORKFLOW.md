# Operator Workflow

## Daily Workflow

1. Confirm the media property and audience focus.
2. Review source signals for sponsors, guests, partners, and collaborators.
3. Score opportunities.
4. Review vault notes.
5. Mark each opportunity `pursue`, `research_more`, or `skip`.
6. Prepare outreach for pursued opportunities.
7. Track outreach lifecycle.
8. Generate report.

## Review Checklist

- Is the target relevant to the audience?
- Is there a real timing signal?
- Is the offer clear?
- Does the pitch preserve trust?
- Are claims accurate?

## Outreach Lifecycle

- `drafted`
- `sent`
- `replied`
- `follow_up_needed`
- `booked_call`
- `closed_won`
- `closed_lost`

## Operating Constraint

Do not automate sponsor, guest, or partner outreach.
