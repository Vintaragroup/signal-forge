# Campaign Plan

## Campaign Summary

- Campaign name: Media Partnership Pipeline
- Audience: sponsors, guests, partners, collaborators
- Offer: sponsorship, guest feature, or collaboration
- Status: draft

## Goal

Create a repeatable media growth pipeline that identifies relevant opportunities and prepares specific human-reviewed outreach.

## Target Filters

- Audience relevance
- Category fit
- Timing signal
- Brand safety
- Contact confidence

## Pipeline Steps

1. Define audience and offer.
2. Import or research sponsor and partner targets.
3. Score opportunities.
4. Review queue.
5. Prepare outreach.
6. Track lifecycle.
7. Generate report.

## Risks

- Weak audience fit
- Overcommercialization
- Unclear offer
- Inflated performance claims

## Human Approval

Required before sponsor, guest, or partner outreach.
