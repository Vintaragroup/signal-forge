# Scoring Rules

## Scoring Goal

A high-scoring media opportunity has strong audience fit, clear commercial or editorial value, credible timing, and a specific next ask.

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Points | Notes |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Audience fit | 30 | relevance to readers, listeners, viewers |
| Opportunity value | 20 | sponsor, guest, partner, distribution |
| Timing signal | 15 | launch, event, campaign, news |
| Brand/editorial fit | 15 | protects trust and quality |
| Contact confidence | 10 | clear person or channel |
| Source confidence | 10 | reliable public evidence |

## Review Rules

- `pursue`: strong audience fit and clear ask.
- `research_more`: audience fit possible, but weak contact or offer.
- `skip`: poor audience fit or brand risk.

## Recommended Offers

- Sponsorship package
- Newsletter placement
- Podcast guest pitch
- Content collaboration
- Event partnership
- Audience distribution swap
