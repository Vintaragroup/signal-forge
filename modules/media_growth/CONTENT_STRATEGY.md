# Content Strategy

## Purpose

Use audience signals and sponsor/partner patterns to create content that grows trust, reach, and commercial opportunity.

## Themes

| Theme | Audience | Signal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Market trend | core audience | news or repeated signals |
| Operator lesson | professional audience | founder or company activity |
| Sponsor-fit education | buyers, sponsors | category interest |
| Guest insight | audience, experts | relevant expertise |
| Event or community recap | partners, audience | event activity |

## Content Types

- Newsletter issue
- Podcast segment
- Short social post
- Sponsor explainer
- Guest briefing
- Partner one-sheet

## CTA Examples

- Subscribe.
- Sponsor an issue.
- Join a collaboration.
- Suggest a guest.
- Request the media kit.

## Approval Notes

Sponsored or partner content should preserve audience trust and be clearly reviewed.
