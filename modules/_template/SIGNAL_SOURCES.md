## Source Types

- Search listings

- Directories

- Job postings
- News mentions
- Event pages
- Review sites
- Existing CRM exports
- Manual research

## Source Rules

- Prefer sources with clear public business context.
- Store source attribution.
- Avoid collecting sensitive personal data.
- Keep raw data local unless explicitly approved.
- Do not add authentication or scraping until scoped.
