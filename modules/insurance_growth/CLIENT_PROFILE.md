# Client Profile

## Client Summary

- Client type: independent insurance agency or specialty broker
- Market: commercial insurance
- Geography: local, regional, or niche national
- Primary offer: business risk review and coverage gap assessment
- Revenue model: policy commissions, renewals, and advisory relationships

## Positioning

The client helps businesses understand operational risk, identify coverage gaps, and prepare for renewal conversations before problems become expensive.

## Ideal Customer Profile

- Small and mid-sized businesses
- Contractors, restaurants, medical offices, manufacturers, property owners, and professional firms
- Companies with hiring, expansion, new locations, new equipment, or compliance-sensitive operations
- Businesses with enough complexity to benefit from an advisor

## Differentiators

- Industry-specific risk understanding
- Responsive advisory process
- Renewal planning
- Practical coverage education

## Constraints

- No automated coverage recommendations.
- No premium estimates in generated copy.
- Human review required before outreach.
- Avoid implying current coverage is inadequate without evidence.
