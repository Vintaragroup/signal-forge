# Outreach Templates

## Initial Risk Review Outreach

```text
Hi, I found {{company_name}} while reviewing {{industry}} businesses in {{location}}.

The signal that stood out was {{risk_signal}}.

If useful, I can share a short commercial insurance risk review checklist for businesses like yours before renewal or expansion decisions.

Worth a quick look?
```

## Renewal Readiness Outreach

```text
Hi, I noticed {{company_name}} appears to be growing in {{location}}.

When teams expand, insurance requirements and certificates can get harder to manage.

I can share a simple renewal readiness checklist if that would be useful.
```

## Follow-Up

```text
Hi, following up on the risk review idea for {{company_name}}.

The reason I thought it might be relevant was {{priority_reason}}.

Should I send the short checklist?
```

## Compliance Rules

- Do not claim coverage is missing.
- Do not estimate premiums.
- Do not recommend specific coverage without licensed review.
- Keep all outreach human-approved.
