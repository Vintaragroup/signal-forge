# Insurance Growth Module

This module defines a SignalForge strategy pack for insurance agencies, brokers, and specialty insurance teams that want to identify commercial prospects, explain risk timing, and prepare human-reviewed outreach.

## Module Focus

- Commercial insurance growth
- Local and niche business prospects
- Coverage-gap and renewal-readiness signals
- Human-approved outreach and follow-up

## Primary Use Cases

- Find businesses with risk or growth signals.
- Score prospects by fit, timing, and data confidence.
- Prepare outreach around practical risk reviews.
- Track review, outreach, follow-up, and pipeline outcomes.
