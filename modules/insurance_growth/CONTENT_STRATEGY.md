# Content Strategy

## Audience

Business owners, operators, and finance leaders who need practical insurance education without jargon.

## Themes

| Theme | Audience | Source Signal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Renewal readiness | owners, CFOs | renewal or growth timing |
| Certificate management | contractors, operators | vendor or contract requirements |
| Hiring and risk | growing businesses | job postings |
| New location checklist | owners, property managers | expansion signals |
| Claims preparedness | operators | risk-heavy industries |

## Content Types

- Checklist posts
- Common mistakes
- Renewal planning notes
- Industry-specific risk explainers
- Questions to ask before expansion

## CTA Examples

- Ask for a risk review checklist.
- Request a renewal readiness conversation.
- Review certificate workflow.

## Approval Notes

All content should be reviewed for compliance and should avoid legal or coverage advice.
