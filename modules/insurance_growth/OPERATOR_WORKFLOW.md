# Operator Workflow

## Daily Workflow

1. Start the stack with `make up`.
2. Run or import source data.
3. Review insurance leads in the vault.
4. Confirm risk and timing signals.
5. Mark each lead `pursue`, `research_more`, or `skip`.
6. Prepare outreach only for pursued leads.
7. Track outreach lifecycle.
8. Generate the report.

## Review Checklist

- Is the company in a target class?
- Is the risk or timing signal real?
- Is the source credible?
- Is the offer compliant and useful?
- Is human review complete?

## Outreach Status Flow

- `drafted`
- `sent`
- `replied`
- `follow_up_needed`
- `booked_call`
- `closed_won`
- `closed_lost`

## Operating Constraint

Do not send automated insurance outreach. Human review is required.
