# Audience Personas

## Business Owner

- Role: owner or founder
- Goal: protect the business while controlling cost
- Pain points: confusing policies, renewals, claims uncertainty
- Buying triggers: expansion, hiring, new contracts, lease changes
- Message angle: practical risk review before renewal or growth

## Operations Manager

- Role: manager responsible for vendor, compliance, or operations risk
- Goal: reduce operational surprises
- Pain points: certificates, requirements, job-site or facility exposure
- Buying triggers: new locations, new crews, vendor requirements
- Message angle: make insurance requirements easier to manage

## CFO Or Controller

- Role: financial decision maker
- Goal: manage risk and budget predictably
- Pain points: renewal increases, unclear coverage value, claims impact
- Buying triggers: renewal window, growth, new assets
- Message angle: coverage review tied to financial planning

## Property Or Asset Manager

- Role: oversees buildings, tenants, or equipment
- Goal: reduce asset and liability risk
- Pain points: tenant requirements, property exposure, vendor risk
- Buying triggers: acquisition, renovation, lease change
- Message angle: risk review for property and vendor exposure
