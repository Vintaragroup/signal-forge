# Signal Sources

## Primary Sources

| Source | Data Collected | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Local business directories | company, category, location, website | finds commercial prospects |
| Job postings | hiring roles, growth area | indicates expansion and operational risk |
| Business registrations | new entities, location | identifies new coverage needs |
| News and press | expansion, funding, contracts | shows timing and risk changes |
| Website pages | services, industries, locations | supports fit and personalization |

## Signal Types

- New business opening
- Hiring growth
- New location
- New vehicles or equipment
- Compliance-heavy service line
- Contracting or vendor requirements
- Renewal timing if known

## Normalized Fields

- `source`
- `source_url`
- `company_name`
- `industry`
- `location`
- `website`
- `risk_signal`
- `timing_signal`
- `data_confidence`

## Source Rules

- Use public business information.
- Store attribution.
- Avoid collecting sensitive personal data.
- Treat all insurance recommendations as human-reviewed.
