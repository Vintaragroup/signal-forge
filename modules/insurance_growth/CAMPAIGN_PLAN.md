# Campaign Plan

## Campaign Summary

- Campaign name: Commercial Risk Review Campaign
- Audience: growing local businesses in target classes
- Offer: short business risk review checklist
- Status: draft

## Goal

Identify and pursue businesses with credible timing or risk signals that justify a practical insurance review conversation.

## Target Filters

- Target industries: contractors, restaurants, clinics, property businesses, professional firms
- Location: defined by operator
- Exclusions: personal lines only, unavailable source attribution, sensitive claims situations

## Pipeline Steps

1. Import or collect business records.
2. Identify risk or timing signals.
3. Score and enrich leads.
4. Review lead notes.
5. Prepare outreach for pursued leads.
6. Track outreach lifecycle.
7. Generate report.

## Campaign Risks

- Compliance-sensitive copy
- Weak signal quality
- Poor contactability
- Overgeneralized outreach

## Human Approval

Required before any outreach is sent.
