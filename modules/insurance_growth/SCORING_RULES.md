# Scoring Rules

## Scoring Goal

A high-scoring insurance lead has clear business fit, a plausible timing or risk signal, enough public data for review, and a practical reason to start a conversation.

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Points | Notes |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Industry fit | 25 | target class or niche |
| Risk or timing signal | 25 | hiring, expansion, renewal, compliance |
| Data quality | 15 | company, website, location, source |
| Contactability | 10 | website, form, public contact |
| Offer relevance | 15 | clear match to risk review |
| Source confidence | 10 | reliable source and attribution |

## Priority Reasons

Good priority reasons should mention:

- Business type
- Specific risk or growth signal
- Why the timing may matter
- Recommended first offer

## Review Rules

- `pursue`: strong fit and credible signal.
- `research_more`: fit is possible but source data is weak.
- `skip`: poor fit, stale source, or compliance concern.

## Recommended Offers

- Commercial coverage gap review
- Renewal readiness check
- Contractor certificate workflow review
- Property or vendor risk review
- Industry-specific risk checklist
