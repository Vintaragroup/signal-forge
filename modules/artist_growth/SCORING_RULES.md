# Scoring Rules

## Scoring Goal

A high-scoring artist opportunity has strong audience or genre fit, good timing, credible contact context, and a clear ask.

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Points | Notes |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Audience fit | 25 | genre, scene, fan overlap |
| Timing | 25 | release, show, campaign moment |
| Contact confidence | 15 | clear target and channel |
| Opportunity value | 15 | press, booking, playlist, partner |
| Source confidence | 10 | reliable public signal |
| Brand fit | 10 | authentic and non-forced |

## Review Rules

- `pursue`: strong fit and timely ask.
- `research_more`: fit is possible but contact or context is weak.
- `skip`: poor genre, audience, timing, or brand fit.

## Recommended Offers

- Release pitch
- Venue booking inquiry
- Playlist or media submission
- Collaboration proposal
- Fan activation campaign
