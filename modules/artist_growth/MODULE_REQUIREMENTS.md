# Artist Module Requirements

## Objective

Help an artist team identify, prioritize, and act on growth opportunities around releases, shows, media, playlists, partnerships, and fan engagement.

## Functional Requirements

- Define the artist profile and brand lane.
- Define target audiences and industry contacts.
- Identify useful public signals.
- Score opportunities by timing, fit, and expected value.
- Prepare human-reviewed outreach.
- Track outreach and follow-up status.
- Generate reports for the operator.

## Non-Goals

- No automated DMs.
- No automated playlist pitching.
- No streaming platform scraping.
- No paid media automation.
- No claims about guaranteed streams, press, or ticket sales.

## Required Data

- Artist name
- Genre or scene
- Market or city
- Current campaign
- Source signal
- Target contact or account
- Recommended ask
- Review status
- Outreach status

## Acceptance Criteria

- Campaign plan is tied to a release, show, or growth objective.
- Outreach templates are specific and respectful.
- Scoring rules explain why an opportunity matters now.
