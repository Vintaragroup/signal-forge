# Campaign Plan

## Campaign Summary

- Campaign name: Artist Release And Opportunity Campaign
- Audience: fans, curators, venues, media, partners
- Offer: release, show, collaboration, or fan activation
- Status: draft

## Goal

Create a focused outreach and content system around a release, show, or growth moment.

## Target Filters

- Genre fit
- Market fit
- Timing fit
- Contact confidence
- Audience overlap

## Pipeline Steps

1. Define campaign moment.
2. Import or research opportunities.
3. Score opportunities.
4. Review queue.
5. Prepare outreach notes.
6. Track replies and follow-ups.
7. Generate report.

## Risks

- Generic pitch
- Weak contact fit
- Overstated traction
- Poor timing

## Human Approval

Required before any outreach or pitch is sent.
