# Signal Sources

## Primary Sources

| Source | Data Collected | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Release calendar | release date, asset status | timing for promotion |
| Venue calendars | venue, date, similar artists | booking and routing fit |
| Social posts | engagement, comments, collaborations | audience and relationship signals |
| Playlist pages | curator, genre, recent adds | pitch relevance |
| Music blogs/newsletters | coverage style, contact | media fit |
| Local event listings | scene activity | market opportunities |

## Signal Types

- New release
- Upcoming show
- Similar artist booking
- Playlist fit
- Press angle
- Collaboration opportunity
- Fan engagement spike
- Market activity

## Normalized Fields

- `source`
- `source_url`
- `artist_name`
- `target_name`
- `target_type`
- `genre`
- `market`
- `signal`
- `timing`
- `contact_confidence`

## Source Rules

- Respect platform rules.
- Do not scrape private data.
- Store source attribution.
- Keep outreach human-approved.
