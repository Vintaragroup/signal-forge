# Content Strategy

## Purpose

Turn release moments, fan signals, and artist story into useful content that supports discovery and relationship building.

## Themes

| Theme | Audience | Signal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Release story | fans, media | new single or project |
| Live moment | fans, venues | show or tour date |
| Creative process | fans | behind-the-scenes |
| Scene connection | local audience | collaborations and events |
| Proof of momentum | partners, media | engagement or placements |

## Content Types

- Release announcement
- Story post
- Behind-the-scenes note
- Show promo
- Fan thank-you
- Media one-sheet
- Partner pitch asset

## CTA Examples

- Listen to the release.
- Save the song.
- RSVP for the show.
- Request the one-sheet.
- Join the mailing list.

## Approval Notes

Content must match the artist voice and should avoid inflated claims.
