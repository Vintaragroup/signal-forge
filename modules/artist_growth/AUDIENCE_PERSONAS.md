# Audience Personas

## Fan Persona

- Role: listener or follower
- Goal: discover music that feels personally relevant
- Pain points: too much generic content
- Buying triggers: new release, live show, behind-the-scenes story
- Message angle: invite them into the artist world

## Venue Buyer

- Role: talent buyer or promoter
- Goal: book acts that fit the room and sell tickets
- Pain points: weak draw, unclear fit, poor promo support
- Buying triggers: tour routing, local audience signal, release campaign
- Message angle: audience fit and promotion plan

## Media Or Playlist Curator

- Role: writer, editor, curator, host
- Goal: feature music their audience will care about
- Pain points: generic pitches, no story, wrong genre fit
- Buying triggers: new release, strong story, local angle
- Message angle: concise pitch with sound, story, and relevance

## Brand Or Community Partner

- Role: partnership lead or community organizer
- Goal: create cultural relevance
- Pain points: misaligned collaborations
- Buying triggers: campaign moment, event, shared audience
- Message angle: authentic collaboration idea
