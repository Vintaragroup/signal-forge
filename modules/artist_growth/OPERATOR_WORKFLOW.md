# Operator Workflow

## Daily Workflow

1. Confirm active artist campaign.
2. Review source signals for venues, media, curators, partners, and fans.
3. Score opportunities.
4. Review notes in the vault.
5. Mark each opportunity `pursue`, `research_more`, or `skip`.
6. Prepare outreach for pursued opportunities.
7. Track outreach status.
8. Generate report.

## Review Checklist

- Is the target aligned with the artist?
- Is the timing tied to a real campaign moment?
- Is the ask clear?
- Is the pitch respectful and specific?
- Are metrics and claims accurate?

## Outreach Lifecycle

- `drafted`
- `sent`
- `replied`
- `follow_up_needed`
- `booked_call`
- `closed_won`
- `closed_lost`

## Operating Constraint

Do not automate DMs, emails, playlist pitches, or booking requests.
