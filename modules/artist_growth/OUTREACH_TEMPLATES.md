# Outreach Templates

## Release Pitch

```text
Hi, I am reaching out about {{artist_name}} and the upcoming release {{release_name}}.

I thought it may fit because {{priority_reason}}.

Here is the short version: {{recommended_offer}}.

Would it be useful to send the private link and one-sheet?
```

## Venue Inquiry

```text
Hi, I saw {{venue_name}} has been booking artists in the {{genre}} lane.

{{artist_name}} is building in {{market}}, and the timing around {{campaign_moment}} could make sense for a show.

Would you be open to reviewing a short booking note?
```

## Follow-Up

```text
Hi, following up on {{artist_name}}.

The reason I thought it could be relevant was {{priority_reason}}.

Should I send the short version?
```

## Rules

- Keep artist voice intact.
- Do not exaggerate metrics.
- Do not mass-send generic pitches.
- Human review required.
