# Artist Growth Module

This module defines a SignalForge strategy pack for music artists, managers, and artist teams that want to identify release, booking, partnership, fan growth, and media opportunities.

## Module Focus

- Music artist growth
- Release and show promotion
- Venue, media, playlist, and partnership opportunities
- Human-reviewed outreach

## Primary Use Cases

- Track signals around releases, shows, markets, and collaborators.
- Score opportunities by timing, audience fit, and contact confidence.
- Prepare pitches for venues, media, playlists, partners, and fan growth campaigns.
- Track replies, booked calls, and campaign outcomes.

## Current Status

Documentation-first module. No music platform, streaming, social, email, or calendar integrations have been added.
