## Positioning

The artist creates a distinct sound and story for a specific audience. Growth work should support authentic fan connection, better release moments, and more relevant industry relationships.

## Ideal Opportunities

- Venues with matching audience
- Local promoters
- Playlist curators
- Music writers and newsletters
- Collaborating artists
- Brands or communities aligned with the artist

## Constraints

- No fake fan metrics.
- No spam pitching.
- No automated outbound.
- Protect artist voice and brand.
- Human review required before outreach.
